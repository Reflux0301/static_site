nullnet.services is back!

Date: 12/23/2023

Thank you for your patience during downtime!

To keep it brief, we've shifted from Proxmox to Windows Hyper-V full time using Fedora CoreOS images.

This has a lot of benefits: better maintainability, ability to provide a slightly reproducible infrastructure (which you can view), better security (up to date Windows with hardened policies instead of a Debian based environment), scalability, easier disaster recovery.

As mentioned, we have cut off support for ABS (Anonymous Browsing Sessions) as it is undergoing a massive refactor, with the goal of moving away from a VPN but routing all traffic through Tor.

I2P is going to be harder to maintain, we've moved to I2PD however it will require manual intervention for restarting/reloading the configuration.

Other than that, downtime has been smooth sailing.
